Services

Water Quality and Waste Management consulting services such as:

  • Wastewater management and water safety plans,
  • Waste Management services relating to SAWIS applications, development of waste management procedures, license applications,
  • Water quality assessment related to the Department of Water and Sanitation's Blue and Green Drop programmes,
  • Bio-monitoring,
  • Waste License applications,
  • Environmental risk assessments,
  • Waste classification and characterization,
  • Aquatic toxicity monitoring.

Ambient Air Monitoring

Imveloenhle Environmental Services provides assistance to customers to meet their legal requirements in terms of the National Environmental Management: Air Quality Act, No.39 of 2004 to monitor air quality:

  • Pollutant Dispersion Modelling,
  • Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA's),
  • Environmental Management Plans (EMP's),
  • Emissions Inventories,
  • Assistance with the National Atmospheric Emissions Inventory System reporting (NAEIS),
  • Atmospheric Emissions License Applications (AEL's),
  • Dust Fallout Monitoring,
  • Environmental noise surveys according to SANS 10103,
  • Particulate and dust matter (PM2.5 and PM10) monitoring.

Company History

Imveloenhle Environmental Services (IES) is 100% black owned enterprise that offers specialised environmental services to the public and the private sector clients in terms of The National Environmental Management Act, No. 107 of 1998 as well as the applicable SEMAs and regulations. We are based in Newcastle Kwa-Zulu Natal, but we hope to expand our services throughout all 9 provinces of South Africa. Imveloenhle Environmental Services offers a range of services such as ambient air and continuous emission monitoring systems which includes data acquisition and reporting. We also offer water quality consulting services and waste management. Imveloenhle Environmental Services is committed to the principles of employment equity, equal opportunity and empowerment, regardless of gender, race, colour or creed. The company acknowledges the need for the safety of its employees at the work place and a healthy environment and is committed to providing this on all sites, in accordance to the Imveloenhle Environmental Services "STOP! THINK! & ACT" philosophy. The aspiration is to make all areas a place of Zero Harm.
